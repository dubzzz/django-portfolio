    What is the aim of this portfolio?

    Every career portfolios' aim is first of all to describe some kind of career path or personal developments through projects. This portfolio is dedicated to people who want to have more details on projects I worked on in the past few years.

    It only shows a subset of projects I worked on until now. Most of them were personal: developed for fun or to ease my everyday life - it is really a waste of time to do several times the same thing when a simple script can do it for you at least as quickly. Others were done during school projects or are related to school materials. Even if I try to be as comprehensive as possible concerning this projects' sample I think that the best way to learn more about them is to ask for details. This portfolio try to give an insight concerning the project itself. Some of these projects can be tested throught executables.

    Keeping an eye on what I have done so far is definitely one of the main justification of this website.
